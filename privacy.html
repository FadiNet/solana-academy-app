Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 — Effective immediately upon use of the app.

Overview

Solana Academy ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. Ever.

Data We Collect

  • Wallet address (public key only — never your private key or seed phrase)
  • Quiz progress and points earned within the app
  • Completed courses and quiz history
  • App language preference (Arabic / English)
  • Anonymous usage data via Firebase Analytics

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Private keys or seed phrases
  • Real name or personal identification
  • Location data
  • Device contacts or media
  • Payment information

How We Use Your Data

  • To save your quiz progress and points across sessions
  • To display your ranking on the leaderboard
  • To mint NFT achievement badges linked to your wallet
  • To improve app content and user experience

Firebase & Third Parties

We use Google Firebase (Firestore) to store app data securely. Firebase's privacy policy applies: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Blockchain transactions (NFT minting) are recorded on the Solana public ledger and are publicly visible by nature.

Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as you use the app. You can request deletion of your account data by contacting us at the email below.
